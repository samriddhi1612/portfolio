Rail Hauler

MECH 223 • Mar 2025 • CAD · Robotics · Manufacturing

Overview

166×40 mm, ~36 g FDM chassis with ±5° front articulation enabling 600 mm curve negotiation and hill clearance. Designed with serviceability and part count in mind.

Highlights

  • Compact geometry optimized for tight curves
  • Articulated front end for improved tracking
  • Low mass; printable jigs for assembly

Tools & Skills

SolidWorks, linkage design, tolerancing, 3D printing setup and QA, basic kinematics checks.

Learnings

Trade-offs between articulation freedom, clearance, and structural rigidity; making quick design iterations with print-time constraints.
